This is Pye's homepage

Pye is a good, old cat.

We inherited Pye when he was 15.

Pye is an anomoly, because of his health.

He is a Siamese cat.

He has:

  • Bad teeth
  • An undifferentiated heart problem
  • Moderate kidney failure
  • Anorexia

The vets he's seen can't figure out why he's still alive.

Because:

  1. His heart condition makes it so we can't treat his kidneys
  2. His kidney condition makes it so we can't treat his heart
  3. It all makes it so he won't eat

But he does because the bad parts also fix the good parts.

The high blood pressure pushes the blood faster through the kidneys, so the impurities don't stick.

The kidneys dehydrate him so less fluid can back up into his chest to bother his heart.
